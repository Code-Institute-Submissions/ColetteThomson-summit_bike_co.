What is a Mountain Bike?

A mountain bike refers to any bike that is designed to be ridden off-road. Here at Summit Bike Co we sell two kinds of mountain bikes: Hardtail mountain bikes are called hardtail because they have a suspension fork at the front and Full Suspension mountain bikes which have a front suspension fork and a rear shock absorber, and are the most accomplished across rough terrain. The mountain bike best suited to you depends on the kind of riding you want to do and, of course, fits into your budget. You can get expert advice on these at any of our Summit Bike Co stores.

What are mountain bike frames made from?

Mountain bike frames are usually made from steel, aluminium or carbon fibre. Wheels are strong with thick tyres and deep treads. The best mountain bike will be one that suits the kind of riding you want to do, at the right price.

What does the 'amount of suspension travel' mean in mountain biking?
