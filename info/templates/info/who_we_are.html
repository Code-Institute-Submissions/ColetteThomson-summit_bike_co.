Who We Are

In 1950, the first shop of Summit Bike Co. opened in Wiltshire, England. Trading from this site for over 20 years, Summit Bike Co. has built up a strong reputation as a quality cycle shop. Our award-winning stores are the heart and soul of our company - the "go-to" place for cyclists of all levels and disciplines.

Most of our store colleagues are avid cyclists themselves, and extensive training ensures the highest levels of technical ability and knowledge. We pride ourselves on giving customers the right information to make informed decisions about their choice of mountain bike.

All stores have areas where you can test ride any of instore bikes. We also have agreements with some of the more popular bike parks for a discounted membership. Please enquire at your local store for more information. We have stores in the South West England, South East England and the greater London area.

Please contact customer services on 0800 333 7777 should you have any questions.

Online Shopping

In 1999 Summit Bike Co. completed its transaction online, ready to adapt to the new world of e-commerce. Since its inception, the Summit Bike Co. website has been in a state of constant evolution, adding features and functionality to keep pace with our customers' sophisticated online shopping needs, whilst at the same time expanding our range to include a comprehensive selection of mountain bikes.
