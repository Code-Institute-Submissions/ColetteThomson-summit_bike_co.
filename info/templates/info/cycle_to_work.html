Cycle to Work Scheme

Are you an employer who is interested in the Ride to Work Scheme?

There are benefits to both employers and employees in partaking in the 'Cycle to Work Initiative'.

Here at Summit Bike Co we know that efficient businesses are often too busy with day-to-day operations and may not have the time or manpower to investigate how the Cycle to Work Scheme could benefit your employees.

Click here to find out more about the UK Government initiative: 'Cycle to Work Scheme Implementation Guidance for Employers' and then contact us so we can assist you in completing all the 'paperwork'.

Step 1 : Sign up with Ride-To-Work
Step 2 : Promote Ride-To-Work to your employees
Step 3 : Approve your employee applications
Step 4 : Salary sacrifice starts - happy employees
